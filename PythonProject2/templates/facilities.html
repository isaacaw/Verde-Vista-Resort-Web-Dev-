Our Facilities

At Verde Vista Resort, we take pride in offering exceptional facilities designed to make your stay unforgettable. From relaxing in our serene spa to enjoying gourmet dining at our in-house restaurants, every detail is crafted with your comfort in mind. Whether you're here for leisure or business, our amenities cater to all your needs, ensuring a perfect blend of luxury and convenient.

Our eco-friendly hotel features sustainable facilities, including energy-efficient meeting rooms, a relaxing swimming pool, a fully equipped gym and more. Each space is designed for comfort and eco-consciousness, providing a green and relaxing experience for every guest.

Swimming Pool

Take a dip in our luxurious pool, perfect for a refreshing swim or lounging. With breathtaking views, poolside cabanas, and a relaxing ambiance, it's an oasis for both relaxation and fun. Open daily from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

Gym / Fitness Center

Stay active during your stay in our fitness center, equipped with the latest cardio machines, free weights and yoga mats. Whether you prefer a vigorous workout or a light stretch, our gym caters to all fitness levels. Open 24/7 for your convenience

Spa

Unwind and rejuvenate at our tranquil spa, offering a range of indulgent treatments, including massages, facials and aromatherapy sessions. Experience pure relaxation in a serene environment designed to refresh your mind and body. Advanced bookings recommended for an enhanced experience.

Ballroom

Host your next event in our elegant ballroom, perfect for weddings, corporate gatherings and special celebrations. With a spacious layout, customizable decor and top-notch audio-visual facilities, our ballroom ensures every occasion is memorable and seamless.
